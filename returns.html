Payment & Order Confirmation

All orders require an advance payment to begin processing. The order is confirmed only after this payment is received. The final balance is due before dispatch, after we send you the final invoice.

Order Cancellation

Cancellation is permitted within 48 hours of placing the order. After this window, orders cannot be canceled as they enter the processing phase. If the final payment is not completed, the order will be canceled and the advance amount will be forfeited.

Returns & Store Credit (No Cash Refunds)

We stand by the quality of our products. Claims for returns are accepted for the following reasons and must be reported within 7 days of receiving your shipment:

  • Major manufacturing defects
  • Damaged goods upon arrival (with unboxing video)
  • Incorrect style or size shipped
  • Missing items from the order

How to File a Claim:

To file a claim, you MUST provide a clear, unedited unboxing video of the sealed package. Claims without a valid unboxing video will not be entertained. Please email your claim to your-email@rooshabhhosiery.com.

Resolution Process:

Upon verification, we will offer a resolution, which may be a replacement, or a store credit note. We do not provide cash refunds. Store credit is valid for 1 year.
